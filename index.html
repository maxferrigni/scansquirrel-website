How It Works

01
Tell Me About Your Shoebox

Maybe it's a mess. Maybe it's just a handful. Maybe it's massive, or all in frames. No matter what you have, it deserves to be preserved. We start with a simple conversation about what you've got.
02
I Scan Everything

Scanning your own photos sucks — it takes forever and never looks right on your phone. I use a professional high-speed scanner at 600 DPI, front and back. Archive-quality, same standard used by museums.
03
AI Enhancement

Every photo gets an AI-enhanced version: colors corrected, faces sharpened, fading reversed. The original scan is always preserved untouched — the enhancement is a bonus, not a replacement.
04
Organized & Safe

The biggest frustration with old photos is never finding them. Your collection comes back cataloged — dated, named, organized by event. Originals in a fireproof case. No more digging through shoeboxes.
05
Enjoyed Forever

Your photos land in your Google Photos or Apple Photos timeline — dated and in order. Face tagging, collages, videos, and more. Need help with any of that? Scan Squirrel can help with that too.
