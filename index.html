Bring Your Old Photos Back To Life

Nice to Meet You!

I'm Max, your neighbor in Eagle Rock. I come to your home and we go through your collection together, organizing your photos into batches by the events and eras of your life. I take them back to my studio and scan and enhance everything within 48 hours. Your originals come back organized in a fireproof, water-resistant case. You get archive-level scans for long-term preservation, and a simple link that drops your enhanced photos directly into Google or Apple Photos.

Amazing Value

  • 600 DPI archival scans of every photo, front & back
  • AI enhanced versions optimized for Google Photos / Apple Photos
  • Embedded IPTC metadata: dates, event names, people
  • Originals returned in a fireproof, waterproof case
  • Archival master scans preserved for future use

Watching the Eaton Fires consume Altadena in 2025 left a permanent mark on me, but what really struck home for me was the loss of hundreds of thousands of irreplaceable photos that I knew likely were never digitized. That experience is why I built ScanSquirrel: to help ensure your memories survive, whether through my service or by sharing the knowledge and tools so you can preserve them yourself.
