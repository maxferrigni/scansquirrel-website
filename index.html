Hi, I'm Max. I live in Glassell Park. In 2025 I watched the Eaton fire tear through Altadena and thought about all the family photos that burned with it — knowing most hadn't been digitized.

That's why I built ScanSquirrel. I take the photos sitting in shoeboxes and turn them into archive-level scans and enhanced versions that go directly into your Google Photos / Apple Photos timeline.

I come to you.

I'm your neighbor and I come to your home. We go through your collection together and organize your photos into batches by the events and eras of your life. I take them back to my studio and scan and enhance everything within 48 hours. Your originals come back organized in a fireproof, water-resistant case. You get a link to your archive-level master scans for long-term preservation, and a ready-to-upload link that drops your enhanced photos directly into your Google Photos or Apple Photos timeline.

I build your archive.

  • 600 DPI archival scans of every photo, front & back
  • AI enhanced versions optimized for Google Photos / Apple Photos
  • Embedded IPTC metadata: dates, event names, people
  • Photos organized by timeline, not scan order

You get your photos secured.

  • Originals returned in a fireproof, waterproof case
  • Enhanced photos delivered directly to your phone and photo apps
  • Face recognition starts working automatically
  • Albums are easy to create and share
  • Archival master scans preserved for future use
