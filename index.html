How It Works

1. We Meet Up

I come to your home and we go through your collection together. We organize your photos into batches by the events and eras of your life — holidays, vacations, school years, whatever matters to you.

2. I Scan Everything

I take your photos back to my studio and scan every single one at 600 DPI — front and back. This is archive-quality, the same standard used by museums and libraries for long-term preservation.

3. AI Enhancement

Each photo gets an AI-enhanced version: colors corrected, faces sharpened, fading reversed. The original scan is always preserved untouched — the enhanced version is a bonus, not a replacement.

4. You Get It All Back

Your originals come back organized in a fireproof, water-resistant case. You get a simple link that drops your enhanced photos directly into Google Photos or Apple Photos — dated and organized, right in your timeline.
