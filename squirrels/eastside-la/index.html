How this started

Why I started Scan Squirrel

The Eaton and Palisades fires in January 2025 terrified me. I watched from right here on the Eastside as neighborhoods burned and people lost irreplaceable photos — wedding albums, baby pictures, the only print of a grandparent. It made me realize how fragile all of it is. I started scanning photos for friends and neighbors just to help. Then more people asked, and it turned into Scan Squirrel — a part-time side hustle doing something I actually care about.

Living on the Eastside, fire risk is something I think about constantly. The hills around us, the dry brush, the Santa Ana winds — it's real. I'm incredibly grateful for the hard work of LAFD and all the agencies fighting to keep our communities safe. A lot of the people who reach out to me fall into two camps — families preserving the photos of someone who's passed away, and people who watched the fires and thought, “I need to protect this stuff before it's too late.” Both of those feel important to me.
