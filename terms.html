01Our Services

Scan Squirrel is a hyper-local photo digitization service based in Eagle Rock, Los Angeles. We pick up your original photographs, scan them at 600 DPI, apply optional AI enhancement, and return everything to you — originals included — along with your complete digital archive.

We are a consumer digitization service. We are not an archival conservator, museum-grade preservationist, or long-term storage provider.

02Quotes & Pricing

All pricing is provided in a written quote before any work begins. Quotes are estimates — final invoices reflect the actual scope confirmed during scanning, with an approximate variance of ±15% depending on final photo counts and material conditions.

Quotes are valid for 30 days. Volume discounts and promotional pricing apply only when explicitly stated in the quote. Billing is per job, invoiced upon completion — not upfront.

03Payment

Payment is due upon delivery of your digitized files and return of your originals. We accept cash, check, Zelle, Venmo, and major credit and debit cards.

A $45 fee applies to returned checks. Scan Squirrel is not responsible for overdraft or overlimit fees resulting from payment processing. If you file a payment dispute (chargeback) with your financial institution and we prevail, the chargeback fee plus a handling fee will be charged back to you.

04Photo Handling Release

Before we accept any materials, every client must sign a Photo Handling & Release Agreement. That document governs the specific terms of handling, liability, damage risk, missing-photo claims, AI enhancement, confidentiality, and dispute resolution for your job — and is incorporated by reference into these Terms.

Among other things, the Release establishes that Scan Squirrel's total liability is capped at the fees paid for the affected job, and that a refund of those fees is your sole remedy.

Haven't signed yet? Contact us at hello@scansquirrel.com before submitting any materials.

05Return & Storage

Your originals come back to you in archival Clear Cases (lettered A–R, roughly 100 photos each), or per the arrangement confirmed in your quote. We strongly recommend the optional fireproof Black Box for long-term storage of returned originals.

After we notify you that your job is complete, we'll hold your originals at no charge for 21 days. After that, a storage fee of $5.00 per day applies. Materials unclaimed after 60 days may be disposed of, and digitized files will be retained for an additional 90 days. Scan Squirrel is not liable for loss following disposal after proper notice.

If you'd like us to dispose of your originals after reviewing your digital archive, written authorization is required. A disposal fee applies per the terms in your quote.

06AI Enhancement

AI enhancement is optional. When selected, it may alter the appearance of images — including color, contrast, saturation, and facial features — using interpretive machine-learning tools. Original unmodified scans are always preserved and delivered alongside any enhanced versions.

Scan Squirrel does not guarantee that AI-enhanced images will meet any particular standard of appearance, and cannot reverse AI processing once a job is complete.
