Meet the Squirrels.

Every Squirrel is a trained, local specialist who handles your collection personally — from pickup to delivery. We're building a network of people who care about this the way we do.

#1 Squirrel · Founder
Max Ferrigni
Eagle Rock, Los Angeles

I started ScanSquirrel after the 2025 Eaton fires. Watching families in Altadena lose decades of irreplaceable photos — overnight, with no warning — made something click. Most of us have shoeboxes sitting in closets right now that would be gone in twenty minutes. I wanted to build something that actually fixed that.

I grew up around cameras and have spent years learning what it takes to do this right — the scanning hardware, the workflow, the AI correction tools, the way to organize a collection so it makes sense to the family who owns it. I handle every job personally, and I bring the same care to your photos that I'd want someone to bring to mine.

Based in Eagle Rock. Glassell Park native. Dad. Neighbor.

What it means to be a Squirrel.

We don't send just anyone. Every Squirrel goes through training, gets the right equipment, and works in a way that earns the trust of the people handing over their most irreplaceable things.

  • → You know your neighborhood. A Squirrel serves the area they live in — people who know the same streets, the same history, the same fire risk. That's not a marketing angle. It's the whole point.
  • → You handle things personally. No hand-offs to a warehouse. You pick up, you scan, you return. Your name is on it.
  • → You care about getting it right. Not just technically correct — actually right. The kind of careful that comes from understanding what these photos mean to the person who owns them.
  • → You follow the ScanSquirrel standard. 600 DPI, proper handling, consistent workflow, honest pricing. Same quality, every job, every Squirrel.
