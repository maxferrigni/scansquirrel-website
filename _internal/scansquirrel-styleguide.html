Capitalization — the one rule

Sentence case everywhere. It matches your voice — direct and human, not brochure-corporate. Capitalize only the first word and proper nouns.

DOMeet the squirrels
DON'TMeet The Squirrels.

No filler eyebrows on page heroes. Don't open a page with a throwaway line above the headline like "Simple and transparent" or "Everything you want to know." The headline should do the work. If an eyebrow doesn't add real information, cut it.

Catalog

Card with teal accent border

Cards: 8px radius, white background, 1px hairline border, optional teal left-accent. Smaller chips/pills: 6px.

Conventions

Em dashes as &mdash; — like this, for breaks in thought.

Middots as &middot; · for separators · in footers and meta lines.

Fonts: Fraunces for all headings, DM Sans for everything else.

Two weights of DM Sans in practice: 400 body, 700 labels & buttons.

Illustrations

The kind of illustrations we use: woodcut/engraving style, ink on bone, monochrome — so the real customer photos are the only color on the page.
