What's always included.

Every job — regardless of size or format — includes all of this.

  • ✓ Local pickup and return. We meet near you — I take your photos, bring them back to my Eagle Rock studio, and return everything to you when it's done. No shipping, no drop-off counter, no strangers handling your memories.
  • ✓ 600 DPI archival scan, front and back. High resolution masters preserved for the long term, not just for sharing on a phone.
  • ✓ AI Photo Correction. For photos that need it — color restoration, scratch and dust removal, clarity improvement for damaged or faded prints. Applied where your photos actually need help, not as a filter on everything. We're adding new corrections all the time.
  • ✓ Dates and names embedded. So your photos land in the right place on your timeline automatically — not in one undated pile from 1983.
  • ✓ Delivery to Google Photos or Apple Photos. One link drops everything onto your phone, organized and ready to share. No thumb drive to lose.
  • ✓ Originals returned organized. Sorted by era or event, labeled, ready to store — physically and digitally.
  • + Fireproof storage case — $40 optional add-on. A waterproof, fireproof case for your originals to live in permanently. Not required, but most people add it. Your photos survived this long — worth protecting going forward.
  • + OCR text recognition — $0.25/page optional add-on. Makes your scanned documents fully text-searchable. Find grandma's recipe, a military record, or any letter by typing a word — not by scrolling through hundreds of files. Worth adding any time documents are part of the job.
