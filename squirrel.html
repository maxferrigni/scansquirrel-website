I am Max, Your LA Neighbor

I am based in Eagle Rock, Los Angeles. Preserving old photos is my passion and my side hustle. I protect and bring your memories back to life.

Watching the Eaton fires consume Altadena in 2025 left a permanent mark on me. What really stuck was knowing hundreds of thousands of irreplaceable photos were gone. Families in the Palisades and Altadena lost everything - and the photos were the part that hurt the most. You can rebuild a house. You cannot rebuild the picture of your dad holding you at the hospital.

That is why Scan Squirrel exists.

A Passion Project, Not a Big Company

Scan Squirrel is not a big operation. It is me, in Eagle Rock, helping friends and neighbors protect what matters most to them. I love this work. I love sitting at someone's kitchen table, going through a shoebox they have not opened in twenty years, and watching them light up when they see their grandparents' wedding photo come back to life.

Because this is a passion project, my prices are very reasonable. I am not trying to build an empire - I am trying to do good work for people I care about, in the neighborhood I live in. The big companies charge a fortune and ship your photos to a warehouse you have never heard of. I live ten minutes away and your photos never leave the neighborhood.

I Take Extremely Good Care

Every photo I scan is irreplaceable. I treat every collection like it is the only copy in the world - because most of the time, it is. Originals come back to you organized, labeled, and protected in a fireproof case. The scans live in three places so nothing is ever at risk of being lost.

I work in your home, on your schedule, at a pace that respects what these photos mean. Nothing gets rushed. Nothing gets lost.

What I Believe

People should protect their photos. That is the whole thing. Whether you hire me or do it yourself, do not wait. Time is not gentle with paper, and disasters do not give warnings.

If you have been thinking about it for years, this is the year. Make it the week.
