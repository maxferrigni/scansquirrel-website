Documents & records

Tax files, medical records, deeds, certificates, the drawer of paper you're afraid to lose — off your desk and into order.

Documents aren't about nostalgia — they're about getting paper handled, fast, and being able to find the one page you need later. We scan quickly and carefully, in color or grayscale, front and back, and turn the stack into clean, organized, searchable files. Add OCR and your paper becomes a searchable PDF you can actually look things up in — or structured data, or whatever format you need it in.

Who's handling these?

A person you know. Not a warehouse of strangers.

Documents are personal in a different way than photos — they hold account numbers, medical history, social security numbers, the details of your life. These are exactly the papers you don't want sitting in an open bin at some far-off facility, handled by people you'll never meet. We do this ourselves, here in the neighborhood, the same person you talked to before you handed anything over. We keep multi-page files in order, name them so they make sense, and treat every page like it matters — because it does.

What we do with documents

Fast turnaround
Documents are about getting paper off your desk. We move quickly — high-speed feeding for stacks of ordinary pages, so a full drawer doesn't become a month-long project.
Color or grayscale
Color when it matters — stamps, seals, signatures, highlighting, letterhead. Crisp grayscale when it's just text, for smaller, cleaner files. We choose per document, or to your preference.
Front and back
Contracts, forms, and records are scanned both sides. Nothing on the back of a page gets left behind.
OCR, your way
Optional OCR turns scanned paper into something you can actually use: a searchable PDF you can look things up in, plain text, or structured data pulled straight off forms — dates, names, amounts. Whatever format the job calls for.
Kept in order
Multi-page documents stay in sequence, named clearly, and grouped by type — taxes, medical, property, certificates — so the digital file is easier to navigate than the original pile ever was.
Delicate & oversized
Fragile, stapled, folded, or larger-than-letter pages go on the flatbed and are handled by hand, not forced through a feeder.
Legal & vital records
Deeds, wills, birth and marriage certificates, passports, titles — handled in person and returned to you intact. The irreplaceable ones never get mailed off to a stranger.
Delivery
Searchable PDFs and image files delivered by secure link, or downloaded straight to your computer. Originals returned to you, organized in the order we found them.
